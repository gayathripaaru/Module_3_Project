OUR SERVICES INCLUDES:

INTERIOR PAINTING

Adding a fresh coat of paint can completely transform the interior of your home. But painting shouldn’t be a chore. That's why Paintzen has simplified the painting experience. With expert painters and quality standards, we can get your job done

EXTERIOR PAINTING

Whether you’re updating your exterior to clean it up or looking to get creative with color and embrace new design trends, painting the exterior of your house will ensure it instantly stands out from the neighbors. It also increases the value of your home!

WALLPAPER INSTALLATION

Wallpaper patterns can bring your walls to life in a way that sometimes paint simply cannot. Wallpaper creates patterns, textures, and unique color combinations. Our crews can install wallpaper, vinyl wall coverings, and decals in your home.

COLOR CONSULTATIONS & PAINT COLOR SAMPLING

Color consultants are here to help you choose the right color for your space. We differ from an interior designer because we use your existing decor and colors to make our suggestions. We have several color consultants on our team that can help you do just that.

CABINET PAINTING

Painting or staining cabinets can be the most effective way to give a kitchen a facelift. Our paint crews are skilled in cabinet painting and staining. We can handle your cabinet painting project, giving you a space that you’ll be proud to spend time in.

DECK STAINING & PAINTING

Painting or staining your deck is a difficult DIY job. Homeowners do paint or stain their decks, but it is difficult. It requires specific tools and experience to do it properly.
